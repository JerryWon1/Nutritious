# Nutritious Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 25, 2026

Nutritious (“the extension”) is a Chrome extension that helps you compare menu items on web pages to your personal nutrition goals.

## What we collect and store

All data stays **on your device** in Chrome’s built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local` and `chrome.storage.sync`). We do **not** operate a backend server and do **not** receive your data on our servers.

Data you may store locally includes:

- Daily macro goals (calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar)
- Diet filter preferences
- Meal log entries (food names and nutrition values you add)
- Optional CSV nutrition rows you import
- Whether the sidebar was left open (local preference)

## What the extension reads from web pages

When you open the sidebar or refresh a scan, the extension reads **visible text and structure** on the **current tab** to detect menu-style item names and, when available, inline calories or nutrition tables. It may also fetch **PDF files linked on the page** (for example, official nutrition PDFs) to extract nutrition hints. This processing happens locally in your browser.

The extension does **not** maintain a history of every site you visit. It only acts on the page you are viewing when you use it.

## What we do not collect

We do **not** collect:

- Name, email, or other account information (no login)
- Payment or financial information
- Precise location or GPS
- Browsing history lists sent to a remote server
- Keystrokes, mouse movements, or analytics telemetry

## Remote code

The extension runs only JavaScript and assets **bundled in the extension package**. It does not download or execute remote scripts.

## Data sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or transfer your data to third parties.

We do **not** use your data for credit decisions or advertising profiles.

Chrome may sync your extension settings if you have Chrome Sync enabled; that is governed by [Google’s privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
